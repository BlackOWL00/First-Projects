Trainer's Methodology I

The Trainers Methodology Level I consists of competencies a TVET trainer or assessor must achieve, such as plan training sessions, facilitate learning sessions, supervise work-based learning, conduct competency assessment, maintain training facilities and utilize electronic media in facilitating training. A person who has achieved this qualification is competent to be a TVET Trainer / Technical Trainer, Training Facilitator / Coordinator and Competency Assessor.

Qualification:Trainer's Methodology I
Duration: 108 HOURS
Requirements:
  • 6 pieces of 1x1 portrait
  • 6 pieces of passport size portrait with collar and nameplate
  • Updated Resume
  • NC II
  • Diploma and TOR/Report Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Baranggay Clearance
  • ID photocopy
